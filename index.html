About Us

Over the past three years during the pandemic, people became very bored because they could not do anything due to lockdowns in different parts of the world. People had to find ways to entertainment themselves to be able to cope with boredom. This is where movies and series payed an important role.

Movies and series provided a platform for people to, as they say, "Kill time". This page is important because it allows individuals to search for any show they dont know and I can assure them they will be able to find it. It is also important because it provide the show's poster with a brief description of the series or movie, the cast and aslo the date for intial release.
